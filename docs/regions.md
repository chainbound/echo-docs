Fiber has endpoints in the following regions:
* us-east-1 (North Virginia)
* us-east-2 (Ohio)
* us-west-1 (North California)
* us-west-2 (Oregon)
* ap-east-1 (Hong Kong)
* ap-northeast-2 (Seoul)
* ap-northeast-1 (Tokyo)
* ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)
* eu-west-1 (Ireland)
* eu-west-2 (London)
* eu-west-3 (Paris)
* eu-north-1 (Stockholm)
