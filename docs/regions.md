Fiber has endpoints in the following regions:

* North Virginia (us-east-1)
* Ohio (us-east-2)
* North California (us-west-1)

#### Asia
* Hong Kong (ap-east-1)
* Tokyo (ap-northeast-1)
* Singapore (ap-southeast-1)

#### Europe
* Frankfurt (eu-central-1)
* London (eu-west-2)
* Paris (eu-west-3)
